SpinDen

Monitor streaming analytics and social media followers from a single platform. Designed specifically for independent musicians.

CASE STUDY

Streaming technology has significantly transformed the music industry, enabling the tracking and analysis of listener behavior and preferences. The emergence of big data metrics has provided valuable insights for music industry executives and emerging artists through comprehensive analytics. SpinDen seeks to differentiate itself from other applications vying for industry standardization by offering an accessible and practical tool designed for musicians across all stages of their careers.

PROBLEM

Musicians often find themselves inundated with data and marketing insights. In the absence of a comprehensive application that consolidates these metrics, they must navigate multiple online platforms to monitor essential statistics such as play counts and follower numbers.

SOLUTION

SpinDen was uniquely created to cater to the typical musician who may feel overwhelmed by extensive data metrics while still requiring straightforward access to their fundamental statistics. Additionally, SpinDen stands out as one of the few mobile applications available, providing users with a vital link to their music's audience reach.

RESEARCH & RESULTS

A user research survey examined several key aspects: preferred streaming platforms, the significance of metrics, tracking rates, and the comparison between desktop and mobile usage.

Seventy-five percent of recording musicians have their music accessible for online streaming. Currently some leading streaming platforms are Spotify, YouTube, and Amazon Music. Additionally, 56% of participants review their streaming metrics on a weekly basis, while none rely solely on mobile devices for this purpose.

A significant number of musicians have their music available for online streaming, although they tend to have a limited catalog. Their interest in metrics is moderate, and they primarily access their accounts through desktop devices.

VIEW REPORT

USER STORIES

User stories ensure a user-centered design approach. By engaging with potential users and understanding their needs, I was able to craft specific narratives that captured their goals and challenges. This process not only clarified the key features required, such as personalized recommendations and real-time data tracking, but also helped prioritize development tasks. By focusing on real user experiences, I aimed to create an intuitive and engaging interface that effectively meets the diverse needs of musicians and listeners alike.

VIEW USER STORIES

USER FLOWS

User flows help visualize and streamline the user experience. These flows helped map out each step a user would take to achieve their goals, from logging in to accessing detailed analytics. By outlining the pathways users would navigate, I was able to identify potential pain points and optimize interactions, ensuring a seamless experience. This structured approach clarified the functionality required and ensured a user-friendly application that effectively serves musicians and listeners.

WIREFRAMES

Wireframes were created to establish a clear visual framework for the user interface. These low-fidelity sketches allowed experimentation with layout, navigation, and content placement without the distraction of detailed design elements. By focusing on the essential structure, I could quickly iterate based on feedback, ensuring that the application was intuitive and aligned with user needs. This early visualization helped communicate ideas before moving into more polished designs.

BRANDING & LOGO

Branding was integrated into the design process to create a cohesive and memorable user experience. By establishing a strong visual identity—through color schemes, typography, and logo design the app aims to resonate with the target audience of musicians. Incorporating brand elements early in the design process ensured that every interface and interaction reflected the mission, ultimately enhancing user connection and loyalty to the platform.

HI-FIDELITY PROTOTYPE

Based on insights gathered from user research, user flows, style guidelines, branding elements, and wireframes, an initial high-fidelity prototype was created. Following further preference testing and multiple design iterations, a more refined mockup was produced.

USABILITY TESTING

A new round of user testing was carried out. The final design iteration included several enhancements: enlarged text size, improved onboarding processes, and the addition of search history features.

CONCLUSION

The SpinDen mobile application originated from a unique concept. I dedicated more time to documenting the creative journey and focused intently on the stages of user testing and design refinement. I discovered that there should be no restrictions on the extent of user testing, as every user contributes significant value to the design process. If I were to advance the SpinDen app further, I would further analysis the onboard experience and incorporate enhanced functionalities within metric analysis. Additionally, I would persist in analyzing the onboarding experience.
